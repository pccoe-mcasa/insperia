TechTank

TechTank is an event where students pitch fully developed software solutions, including web apps, mobile apps, or desktop software, to a panel of expert judges. Participants will showcase their complete projects and demonstrate innovation, technical execution, and market viability. It's an opportunity to gain recognition, mentorship, and support for further development

Event Rules and Structure

  • Eligibility: Open to Undergraduate(UG) and PostGraduate(PG) Students
  • TeamComposition: Teams must consist of individual or 1-4 members (individual participation is allowed).
  • Software: Participants must present a complete, functional application (web, mobile, or desktop).
  • Pitching Format: Teams will present their software in a 5-7 minute pitch followed by a Q&A session with judges.
  • Judging: Projects will be evaluated on innovation, technical execution, design, market potential, and pitch quality .

Presentation Rules

  1. 1. Time Allocation: Eachteam gets 10 minutes for presentation, followed by 5 minutes for Q&A
  2. 2. Medium: Presentations includes live demonstrations of the software.

Code of Conduct

  1. 1. All participants are expected to maintain professional decorum during the event.
  2. 2. Failure to adhere to submission deadlines will result in disqualification.
