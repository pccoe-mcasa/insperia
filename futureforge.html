FutureForge

FutureForge is an innovation-focused event aimed at showcasing computer application solutions that address critical challenges in the Defence Tech and Sports and Fitness domains. Teams will develop and present fully functional software solutions — web apps, mobile apps, or desktop software — designed to enhance national security, improve physical health, or boost sports performance. This event encourages creative problem-solving, technical expertise, and impactful software development, offering participants a chance to gain recognition, mentorship, and opportunities for future collaboration.

General Rules

  • Eligibility: Open to Undergraduate(UG) and postgraduate(PG) students specializing in computer applications or related fields.
  • TeamComposition: participation is allowed in individual and teams of 1 to 4 members (individual participation is allowed).
  • Domains of Innovation:
    1. Defence Tech: Focus on innovations for national security, including surveillance, threat detection, secure communication, and military technology solutions.
    2. Sports and Fitness:
    3. Fitness: Solutions that improve physical and mental health, track progress, and motivate users, possibly leveraging gamification or AI-driven insights.
    4. Sports: Innovations related to athlete performance, coaching, fan engagement, or sports equipment.
    5. Other Domains: Participants are welcome to propose solutions for other domains as well.

Submission Rules

  1. 1. Abstract Submission: A1-pageabstract clearly defining the problem statement, proposed solution, and expected outcomes is mandatory.
  2. 2. Final Report: Adetailed report (up to 10 pages) containing problem statement, proposed solution, technology stack, system architecture, implementaion progress, and expected final prototype.
  3. 3. Prototype Submission: Teamsmust provide a functional prototype during the presentation

Presentation Rules

  1. 1. Time Allocation: Eachteam gets 10 minutes for presentation, followed by 5 minutes for Q&A
  2. 2. Medium: Presentations includes live demonstrations of the software.

Code of Conduct

  1. 1. All participants are expected to maintain professional decorum during the event.
  2. 2. Failure to adhere to submission deadlines will result in disqualification.
